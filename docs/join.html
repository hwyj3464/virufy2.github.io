Join the fight to stop COVID

Virufy is a not-for-profit organization that is passionate about making an impact on the world. We foster a positive learning environment with a focus on peer mentorship to allow volunteers to grow in their field and use their skills to make an impact. Our volunteers contribute remotely to our mision. You can be based anywhere worldwide unless otherwise noted on the role description.

People

Youth Ambassador

We are seeking students who can establish a campaign so that as many people as possible in their local or regional area are regularly using Virufy's web and smartphone apps to provide their cough data.

Project Manager

We are seeking a project manager who will work closely with the development team in order to define a product roadmap, manage development progress and align with stakeholders.

Business/Finance Manager

This person will contribute on a part-time volunteer basis and collaborate closely with the management and development team to help manage the overall business, product, and finances.

Fundraising Coordinator

This person will contribute on a part-time volunteer basis and collaborate closely with the development team, define the product roadmap, manage development progress and align with stakeholders.

Engineering

UX Researcher / Designer

We are looking for a UX researcher passionate about designing user-friendly web and mobile apps. You will be collaborating with our engineers to develop and maintain high quality mobile-friendly applications to be used by millions of people.

Website Developer

We are looking for a qualified website developer to join our Engineering team. Our team of volunteers develop and maintain our website, which can be seen by hundreds of millions of people. If you’re passionate about frontend web development and helping people around the world, we would like to meet you.

AI Engineer

Our team of volunteers works with machine learning, deep learning, and signal analysis for audio analysis in a medical setting. We welcome students and professionals with experience in these domains.

Graphics Illustrator

We are looking for a graphic designer to contribute on a part-time volunteer basis who is passionate about creating digital content, illustrations, and branding assets. You will be collaborating with our marketing team and web development team to create digital content on our website and social media.

Medical

Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC)

We're seeking individuals to support in coordinating clinical activities. The CRC will be involved in preparing research protocols, reporting, informed consent, conduct of research, safeguarding PHI, and some general administrative matters.

Legal

Data Privacy Manager

This person will contribute on a part-time volunteer basis and collaborate closely with the management and development team to help manage the overall data privacy concerns internally, potentially under mentorship from senior licensed lawyers.
